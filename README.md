### Specification Overview
Vinyl casement window factory pricing for B2B procurement is driven by four primary components: frame profile, glass package, hardware grade, and sash reinforcement.

### Frame Profile & Wall Thickness
- **Standard Residential**: 1.5 mm wall, 3‑chamber (DP R25)
- **Mid‑Range Commercial**: 1.8 mm wall, 5‑chamber (DP R40)
- **Premium High‑Performance**: 2.0 mm wall, 7‑chamber with foam fill (DP R50)

### Glass Options & Cost Impact
| Type | U‑Factor | SHGC | Cost per Window (50+ qty) |
|------|----------|------|--------------------------|
| Double Low‑E Argon | 0.30 | 0.35 | $85–$105 (std) / $115–$145 (mid) |
| Double Low‑E Argon + warm‑edge spacer | 0.25 | 0.28 | $115–$145 |
| Triple Low‑E 272 Krypton + laminated inner | 0.18 | 0.22 | $165–$220 |

### Hardware Grades
- **Standard**: zinc die‑cast, ~$12–18/unit
- **Mid‑Range**: stainless steel, 25,000 cycles
- **Heavy‑Duty**: stainless steel, 50,000 cycles, ~$25–35/unit

### Container Pricing (FOB China)
- 20′ container ≈ 120 units; 40′ container ≈ 260 units
- Mid‑grade unit price: $115–145 → 40′ total: $29,900–$37,700
- Landed cost (incl. freight & duties): ~$152–158/unit (mid‑grade)

### Cross‑Border Procurement Checklist
1. Specify NFRC‑certified U‑factor, SHGC, VT — not just glass type.
2. Require AAMA/WDMA/NAFS test reports for air infiltration (≤0.3 cfm/ft²), water penetration, and structural performance.
3. Lead time: 30–45 days design → ship, plus 15–20 days customs/inland.
4. Obtain hardware samples for 3–5 complete units before production.

### Installation Cost Breakdown (per window)
- Rough opening prep: $15–25
- Flashing/sealants: $8–12
- Trim/finishing: $10–20
- Labor: $80–150
