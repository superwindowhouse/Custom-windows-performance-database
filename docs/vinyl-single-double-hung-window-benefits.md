# Vinyl Single and Double Hung Window Engineering Specification

## Overview
This document specifies the engineering characteristics and performance parameters of vinyl single and double hung windows for commercial and residential applications, based on AAMA 101-certified designs with vertical sliding operation and balanced sash systems.

## Performance Parameters

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| U-Factor | ≤ 0.30 Btu/h·ft²·°F (AAMA 101 certified) |
| Design Pressure (DP) | DP-35 or DP-50 (optional) |
| Forced Entry Resistance | ASTM F588 (10‑lb drop test on latch) |
| Impact Resistance (optional) | AAMA 1304‑15 |

## Material Configuration

- Frame: Vinyl (PVC) extrusion, full‑thickness color throughout profile
- Sash: Vinyl (PVC) with continuous sash interlock (PVC profile locking meeting rails full width)
- Sill: Steel‑reinforced (optional for DP-50)
- Locking system: Multi‑point (3 or 4 points along meeting rail) or single‑center latch (standard grade)
- Weatherstripping: Replaceable (service life 15–20 years)
- Insect screens: Available on both sashes (tilt‑in double hung)
- Balance system: Block‑and‑tackle, replaceable
- Finishing: Factory‑installed nailing fins and brickmold (full assembly)

## Structural Characteristics

- Vertical sliding operation with balanced sash system reduces mechanical wear
- Only bottom sash moves in single hung; both sashes tilt inward for cleaning in double hung
- Upper and lower sashes operate independently for ventilation control
- Continuous sash interlock provides full‑width engagement at meeting rails
- Steel‑reinforced sill prevents bowing under forced lifting pressure
- Lighter sash weight compared to wood or aluminum reduces installation labor

## Thermal / Acoustic / Energy Performance

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| U-Factor | < 0.30 Btu/h·ft²·°F |
| Air Infiltration | Meets AAMA 101 requirements (no numeric value stated) |
| Energy Star | Most Efficient threshold compliance |

## Applicable Standards

- AAMA 101 (window certification)
- ASTM F588 (forced‑entry resistance, 10‑lb drop test)
- AAMA 1304‑15 (impact resistance for high‑security projects)
- NFRC labeling (U-value and SHGC verification)

## Engineering Notes

- Vinyl material is moisture‑resistant; does not warp, swell, or corrode.
- No on‑site painting, staining, or corrosion treatment required over 20‑year lifecycle.
- Single hung recommended when exterior access is available for cleaning; double hung when both sashes must tilt inward.
- Custom sizes require frame reinforcements specified at design stage to match structural test reports.
- For large‑scale projects (50‑unit apartments or 200‑home subdivisions), specify full assemblies with factory‑installed nailing fins and brickmold to eliminate field fabrication.
